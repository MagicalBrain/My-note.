由此可知 $f^{\prime}(0)=f^{\prime}-(0) \stackrel{\prime}{=} f^{\prime}+(0)=1 .$ 应选(D).

$$

&=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\int_{-1}^{2 x} e^{2^{2}} d t-x}{x^{2}}=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{2 e^{t x^{2}}-e^{x^{2}}-1}{2 x}=0
\end{aligned}

$$
\begin{array}{c}{R= \frac{1}{k} = \frac{\left(1+y^{\prime 2}\right)^{\frac{3}{2}}}{\left|y^{\prime \prime}\right|}}\end{array}
$$
