regard

## 长难句

Frequent use of a mobile phone in the condition of high temperatures can damage it. 

经常在高温条件下使用手机会对其造成损伤。

Any damage to your smart phone's screen will affect the content display.

你的智能手机屏幕的任何损坏都会影响到内容的显示。
